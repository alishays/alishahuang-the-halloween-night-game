Welcome to The Halloween Night.

At the Halloween night....

the little girl, Sandy, woke up, she found that she is in a room that she does not know when she looked around the room. This thing freaked Sandy out, so she ran to the door and tried to open the door. However, she founded the door is locked up by an electronic door lock, and she does not know the code for the lock. Also, there is no other exit in the room. Therefore, You will be the little girl, and You needs to find clues in the room to escape the room that locked you up.
